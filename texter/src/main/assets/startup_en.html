Hello, GPS Texter

The program calculates distance from your home. Each time your distance is reduced by, or grows by 2 km, it sends a text message.

Your carrier may charge you for each text message you send. It is advised to use this program if you have free text messages in your price plan.

You may now dismiss this window or go directly to the settings menu by pressing a button below (recommended). You can invoke settings later by tapping the cogwheel () in the upper right corner of the screen. Start using the program by entering the phone number and your home location.

The program is available on GitHub: https://github.com/syrop/GPS-Texter
